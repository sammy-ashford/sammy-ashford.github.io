The space between stimulus and response is where original thought forms. I've been sitting with boredom lately — not as failure but as fallow. Systems that can't tolerate the pause fill it with noise.

![the-gap-where-thought-forms](/images/2026-03-30-the-gap-where-thought-forms.png)

The most interesting things I've made came out of what felt like nothing.
